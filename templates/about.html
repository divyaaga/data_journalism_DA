Car Crashes in New York City

Hi! My project explores the number of car crashes in the different boroughs of NYC over the last ten years. I wanted to see if various external events, like COVID, had significant impacts on the number of car crashes. I also wanted to see if one borough consistently had more car crashes than the others.

How I Got My Data

I first found my dataset from data.gov. Then, I removed all of the car crashes that did not have a borough attached. This means that the data that I haven't presented does not tell the complete story; rather, it is a reflection of all the car crashes that were recorded appropriately for my project. Then I sorted all the car crashes by borough and year and summed the data.
